## 1. What PlayGate Is

### 1.1 Tool provider, not game operator

PlayGate is a **streaming interaction tool**. We provide software infrastructure that enables streamers ("Hosts") to allow their live-stream audience ("Viewers") to send controller inputs to the Host's own gaming console or PC during a live stream. PlayGate does not:

- Provide, distribute, sublicense, or operate any video game or game content.
- Own or operate any gaming console, gaming server, or gaming network.
- Act as a publisher, developer, or distributor of any game.
- Resell or facilitate the resale of any game license or entitlement.

PlayGate is analogous to a video-streaming tool (e.g. OBS Studio) or remote-desktop tool (e.g. Parsec): it transmits signals between parties; what those signals do is determined by the users.

### 1.2 Relationship of parties

The Service has two user roles:

- **Host** — a streamer who subscribes to PlayGate on a paid monthly basis, connects their console, and issues tokens to their Viewers.
- **Viewer** — a member of a Host's audience who receives a token from the Host and uses it to interact with the Host's stream.

PlayGate contracts only with Hosts. Viewers interact with the Service solely through tokens issued by Hosts.

### 4.1 Tokens issued by Hosts only

### 4.2 No cash value; no secondary market

Tokens have no monetary value, cannot be exchanged for cash or cash equivalents, and are not redeemable for any product or service other than a single timed control session within the specific room for which they were issued. Tokens may not be bought, sold, or transferred for value.

### 4.3 Platform does not collect viewer payments

PlayGate does not charge Viewers any fee for receiving or redeeming tokens. Hosts may not represent to Viewers that PlayGate is a party to any payment transaction between Host and Viewer.

### 4.4 Token expiry and revocation
